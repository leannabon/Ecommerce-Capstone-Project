    Our mission

    Weird Instruments is a website dedicated to exploring and celebrating unique and unusual musical instruments from around the world. The site features articles, videos, and reviews on a wide range of strange and fascinating instruments, from the theremin and glass harmonica to the hydraulophone.

    Visitors to the site can browse a variety of categories, including wind instruments, percussion instruments, string instruments, electronic instruments, and more. Each instrument is accompanied by a detailed description of its history, design, and playing techniques, as well as examples of notable performers who have used it in their music.
